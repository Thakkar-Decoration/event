Types of Events

Welcome

A welcome event is organized to greet and introduce newcomers or guests to a particular place, group, or occasion.

Together

A together event emphasizes unity, collaboration, or shared experiences. It could be a team-building activity, a community gathering, or any occasion where people come together to bond, celebrate, or work towards a common goal.

Opening get

An opening get-together marks the beginning of an event, such as a conference, exhibition, or festival.

Mehandi Ceremony

The Mehandi ceremony is a traditional pre-wedding ritual in many cultures, particularly in South Asia. It involves applying henna designs to the bride's hands and feet, symbolizing beauty, joy, and auspiciousness.

Haldi Ceremony

The Haldi ceremony is another pre-wedding ritual commonly observed in Indian weddings. It entails applying turmeric paste to the bride and groom's skin, signifying purification, protection from evil, and enhancing their glow for the wedding day.

Birthday Celebration

A birthday celebration commemorates the anniversary of a person's birth. It's a joyous occasion where friends and family gather to honor and celebrate the individual, often with gifts, cake, and various festivities.

Baby Shower

A baby shower is a party held to celebrate the pending birth of a child and to offer gifts to the expectant mother. It's a time for friends and family to share in the excitement and anticipation of the new arrival.

Anniversaries

Anniversaries mark the passage of time in a relationship, whether it's a marriage, partnership, or even a business venture. Couples celebrate their journey together and reflect on their achievements and growth.
